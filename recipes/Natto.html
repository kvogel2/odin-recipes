Natto

Description:

Natto is a japanese cuisine that is often eaten with rice and other things. Natto is fermented soy beans. Natto is one of Kai's favorite foods. Natto is incredibly divisive in Japan. Half the country hates it and half the country loves it. I will admit that it has an odd smell and if I wasn't eating it since I was a baby I maybe wouldn't love it. But that's not how life unfolded.

Ingredients:

  • Soy beans
  • Egg Yolk
  • Fermentation
  • Soy sauce
  • Mustard??
  • Bacteria
  • Rice

Steps:

  1. Grab the natto box
  2. Remove the sheet on top
  3. Put the soy sauce and the mustard in the beans
  4. Crack an egg and remove the whites
  5. Put the raw egg yolk in the beans
  6. Mix em with some chopsticks
  7. Enjoy the stringey goodness!
